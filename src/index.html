About Cloudy Shop ☁️

Cloudy Shop was founded on a simple premise: what if a store had the reliability of a well-architected cloud system, but also sold cool stuff? We auto-scale our inventory, geo-replicate our warehouses, and deploy our shipping labels via a fully automated CI/CD pipeline.

Our team is fully distributed (across three continents and two couch cushions), and we practice extreme agility — our stand-ups happen while actually standing up. We believe in eventual consistency: eventually, you will be happy with your purchase. Guaranteed.

Our tech stack is powered by the spirit of the cloud: elastic, ephemeral, and occasionally inexplicably unavailable for 30 seconds before coming back as if nothing happened.
